Welcome to MS Fit!

MS Fit is an exercise and fitness community for people with Multiple Sclerosis that meets every Saturday at 10AM - 11:30AM. Lindsay Purcell and Nick Purcell started MS Fit & Friends in January of 2015 with the intent to help those with MS in the Houston area exercise at the Metropolitan Multi-Service Center (MMSC). The MMSC is a free service and therefore MS Fit is free to all registered members of the MMSC.

Mission Statement: To provide a safe and fun environment for people with MS to participate in group exercise and improve members knowledge and self-efficacy regarding exercise participation.
